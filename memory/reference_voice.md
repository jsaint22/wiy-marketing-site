Key rules:
- One-sentence test: could any other advisor have written this? If yes, rewrite.
- Forbidden phrases: "holistic", "synergy", "wealth management solutions", "unique value proposition", "secret nobody's telling you", "should" directed at clients
- Use: "design your life", "ideal life", "flat fee", "fiduciary", "I've seen this before"
- Tone: direct, conviction-first, short sentences, human before professional
- As seen in: Forbes, MarketWatch, Advisorpedia ONLY (verified three)
