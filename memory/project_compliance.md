description: Required disclosures, restrictions on testimonials and performance claims
type: project
---

Required footer disclosure: "Wealth In Yourself is an Investment Adviser registered in Nevada. Advisory services provided to clients nationwide where permitted."

Restrictions:
- No testimonials unless compliance-cleared with disclosures
- No performance claims
- No forward-looking performance statements
- Case studies must include hypothetical disclaimer
- Form CRS (ADV Part 3) and Privacy Policy must be linked

**Why:** SEC/state regulatory requirements for registered investment advisers.
**How to apply:** Check all new pages and blog posts against these rules before publishing.
